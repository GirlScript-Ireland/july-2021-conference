Aida Oghabi

Born and raised in Stockholm, Aida Oghabi is a Senior Account Manager at Google with over eight years of experience in Digital Marketing and recently announced as one of the top ten talents in the vertical 'Retail, Sales & Marketing' by the Swedish Nova 111 List. Throughout her career, Aida has been working with large global enterprises such as the L'Oréal Group, Unilever and the Fiat Group and large Nordic e-Commerce clients that have grown to become unicorn companies. While working at Google, Aida is running an e-learning company within the creative industry, lectures at some of the top business schools in Sweden and volunteers as a mentor and coach for younger females in the tech industry.

Ashish Kumar

Ashish is a techno-manager currently working with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) & has also worked for renowned companies like Accenture, Nokia, Jakson Solar & Su-Kam Solar. He is a well-known speaker in the area of green startups, career growth, brand building, industry-specific supply chain and has been invited by over 100 colleges in India including the IIMs, IITs, DU and top Engineering colleges as a guest speaker or event judge. Ashish is the creator of Everyday Innovators (an innovation & consulting forum, selected as one of the potential startups under Bihar government startup policy) supporting start-ups, ventures and students. He is also a mentor for multiple social projects at different colleges in Delhi and has trained over thousands on solar startup opportunities.

Bobby Healy

Bobby founded Eland Technologies (an airline technology company) which he sold in 2003 to SITA.aero. He then built CarTrawler over 14 years – the world’s largest mobility marketplace for airlines, and led two successful LBOs for the business. For the last 3 years, he’s been building ‘Manna’ - a “Drone Delivery as a Service” business for the world with which he plans to revolutionise the world of online food, grocery and pharmacy delivery – making a 3 minute, low-cost delivery service as pervasive as running water

Vaishnavi Sudha

Maurice Knighty

Maurice Knightly is an Entrepreneurial Specialist at UCD’s Innovation Academy and leads on the Undergraduate and Transversal Skills programmes. He is a non-executive director of several companies ranging from food and catering to new media. From 1992 to 2009 he was Operations and Training Director of the O’Brien’s Irish Sandwich Bars franchise, responsible for operations, training, marketing and purchasing. He worked to grow the chain to 282 outlets in 10 countries and developed the brand’s initial presence in each new market. Maurice has served on many boards and panels including Retail Excellence Ireland and Food Safety Authority of Ireland and he has mentored both privately and with Local Enterprise Offices and Enterprise Ireland. He has always been active in the charity and social enterprise sector and has raised €1.25M for the 2003 Special Olympics World Games and €.5M for the Christina Noble Children’s Foundation through O’Brien’s.

Bukky John

Bukky John is an optimist and enthusiastic lifelong learner. She qualified as a mental health nurse after graduating with first class honours from Trinity College Dublin. She has extensive experience in both inpatient and community mental health services across a variety of settings in the UK and Ireland, including adult, child and adolescent, and psychiatry of later life. She is passionate about health and wellbeing, growth & personal development, and spirituality. Bukky is a Certified Integrative Nutrition Health Coach & Certified Life Coach and is an advocate of using holistic approaches in addressing patient healthcare needs. She is hugely committed to women empowerment and encouraging each individual to follow their highest self-expression and purpose.
